Engine: It features a 648cc, air-cooled, SOHC, fuel-injected parallel-twin engine.
Power: This engine generates around 47 horsepower (35 kW) at 7250 rpm.
Torque: The torque output is approximately 52 Nm at 5250 rpm.
Transmission: It comes with a 6-speed gearbox, offering smooth shifts and flexibility across various riding conditions.
Cooling: The engine employs air cooling, which is a traditional method but efficient for this bike's purpose.
Fueling: It's equipped with fuel injection, providing better fuel efficiency and throttle response compared to carbureted systems
Acceleration: The GT 650 offers brisk acceleration, especially in the mid-range, thanks to its torquey parallel-twin engine. It can reach highway speeds swiftly and comfortably.
Handling: With its café racer-inspired design and sporty ergonomics, the GT 650 handles well through corners and urban environments. The chassis provides a stable platform for confident riding, while the suspension setup offers a balance between comfort and sportiness.
Braking: Equipped with disc brakes at both ends and possibly with ABS (depending on the model and region), the GT 650 offers reliable stopping power. The brakes provide progressive feedback, enhancing rider confidence
The curb weight (including all fluids and a full fuel tank) of the GT 650 typically falls in the range of 198 kg to 202 kg (approximately 436 lbs to 445 lbs).
The GT 650 comes with a wet, multi-plate clutch system, which is durable and provides smooth engagement. It offers a light clutch pull, making it easy to operate in stop-and-go traffic or during spirited riding
Standard Continental GT 650

This is the base model of the Continental GT 650 lineup. It typically comes with standard features and finishes, providing the core GT 650 experience without additional frills or accessories. This version offers the classic café racer style and performance that the GT 650 is known for..

Custom or Special Editions

Occasionally, Royal Enfield releases special edition versions of the GT 650. These custom or special editions may feature unique paint schemes, graphics, and sometimes additional accessories. These editions often cater to enthusiasts looking for a more customized or distinctive appearance.
